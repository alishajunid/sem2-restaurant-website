Our Food Boxes

The Veggie Delight Box

The Veggie Delight Box celebrates the goodness of plant-based eating with a diverse range of seasonal vegetables and grains. This box includes a bunch of kale, a head of cauliflower, a pound of green beans, a pound of mushrooms, a pound of tofu, a can of chickpeas, and a bag of quinoa, inspiring you to create creative and nutextures, and flavors of fresh produce.

The Family Feast Box

The Family Feast Box is a curated selection of ingredients to create a hearty and wholesome dinner for the whole family. This box features four tender chicken breasts, a pound of ground beef, a pound of pasta, a jar of marinara sauce, a head of broccoli, a bag of mixed salad greens, and a loaf of bread, providing everything you need to prepare a memorable and satisfying meal.

The Fresh Start Box

The Fresh Start Box is a vibrant assortment of fruits and vegetables designed to kickstart your day with nutritious options. Each box includes four crisp apples, four juicy oranges, two ripe bananas, a bunch of fresh spinach, a head of lettuce, a cucumber, a bunch of carrots, and a bunch of celery, all sourced from local farmers and markets to ensure freshness and quality for your meals.

The Protein Power Box

The Protein Power Box is designed to fuel your active lifestyle with high-quality meats, poultry, and seafood. This box features four salmon fillets, four pork chops, a pound of shrimp, a pound of chicken thighs, a pound of lean beef steak, a dozen eggs, and a gallon of milk, all sourced from local farms and fisheries committed to ethical and sustainable practices, ensuring you have the essential building blocks for delicious meals.

The Quick & Easy Box

The Quick & Easy Box simplifies mealtime with a curated selection of pre-prepped ingredients and ready-to-cook meals. This box includes a pre-marinated chicken, a bag of pre-cut stir-fry vegetables, a package of pre-cooked rice, a jar of stir-fry sauce, a loaf of pre-baked garlic bread, a bag of pre-washed salad greens, and a bottle of salad dressing, offering a convenient and delicious solution for busy individuals and families.

The Seasonal Surprise Box

The Seasonal Surprise Box celebrates the flavors of each season with a rotating selection of fresh and unique ingredients inspired by local harvests. Each box is tailored to the season, featuring an assortment of fruits, vegetables, herbs, and specialty items that showcase the best of what nature has to offer throughout the year. Whether it's juicy summer berries, crisp autumn apples, hearty winter greens, or vibrant spring produce.
