Volunteer With Us


At Welcome Home, we believe that everyone should have access to fresh, nutritious food, regardless of their financial situation. If you are struggling to afford food, we want you to know that you are not alone, and we are here to help. In addition to providing nourishing meals and food boxes through our “Pay As You Feel” model, we offer opportunities for you to contribute your skills and time in exchange for food box collection.

Monetary Donations:


If you would like to support our mission but are unable to volunteer, we also welcome monetary donations either in-store at the time of service or online through our website. Your generous contributions help us continue our efforts to combat food waste, provide nourishing food to those in need, and create a more sustainable and compassionate community for all. Join us in making a difference and become a part of something bigger than yourself. Together, we can build a brighter and more inclusive future for everyone.
